# Interactive Checklist Walkthrough Demo

## Step-by-Step Process for Creating a Blog Post with Interactive Elements

### 1. Access the CMS
- Navigate to `/cms/login` 
- Use admin credentials to log in
- You'll see the CMS dashboard with existing posts

### 2. Create New Blog Post
- Click "Create New Post" button
- Fill in basic post information:
  - **Title**: "Is Your Business Ready for Automation? Take Our Assessment"
  - **Meta Title**: "Business Automation Readiness Assessment | Free Interactive Tool"
  - **Meta Description**: "Evaluate your business automation needs with our comprehensive 3-phase assessment. Get personalized recommendations and priority scoring."
  - **Slug**: "automation-readiness-assessment"
  - **Category**: "Business Strategy"
  - **Tags**: automation, assessment, business-tools, efficiency

### 3. Write Content with Interactive Element
In the content editor, add this markdown:

```markdown
# Is Your Business Ready for Automation? Take Our Assessment

As businesses grow, manual processes that once worked efficiently can become major bottlenecks. But how do you know when it's time to automate? Our interactive assessment tool helps you evaluate your automation readiness across three critical phases.

## Why This Assessment Matters

Before investing in automation solutions, it's crucial to understand:
- Which processes are causing the most friction
- Where your team is spending valuable time on repetitive tasks
- What the potential impact of automation could be for your business

## Complete Your Automation Readiness Assessment

Take 5-10 minutes to complete our comprehensive evaluation below. You'll receive personalized recommendations and priority scoring based on your responses.

[interactive type="automation-checklist"]

## What Happens After Your Assessment

Based on your assessment results, you'll receive:
- **Priority Level**: Understanding of your automation urgency (Low/Moderate/High)
- **Personalized Report**: Detailed analysis of your current state
- **Next Steps**: Recommended actions based on your specific situation
- **Free Consultation**: Opportunity to discuss your results with our automation experts

## Ready to Take Action?

If your assessment indicates high automation potential, our team can help you develop a strategic implementation plan. [Book a free consultation](/#contact) to discuss your specific needs and explore how automation can transform your business operations.
```

### 4. Use the Interactive Content Picker
- In the content editor, you'll see an "Add Interactive Element" button
- Click it to open the interactive content library
- Select "Automation Readiness Checklist"
- Click "Insert" to add the shortcode at your cursor position

### 5. Configure Post Settings
- **Status**: Published
- **Author**: Select appropriate team member
- **CTA Type**: Consultation (matches the post content)
- **Featured Image**: Upload relevant automation/assessment image
- **Reading Time**: Auto-calculate or set manually

### 6. Preview and Publish
- Use the Preview button to see how the interactive element renders
- The checklist will appear as a professional assessment tool
- Publish when satisfied with the content

### 7. Test the Interactive Element
- Visit the published post URL
- Complete sections of the assessment
- Verify progress tracking works
- Test the report generation feature
- Confirm lead tracking is functioning

This creates a complete blog post that provides value while generating qualified leads through the interactive assessment tool.
