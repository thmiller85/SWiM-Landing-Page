# Interactive Checklist Integration Strategy

## Strategic Placement in the Blog Post

### 1. Early Engagement (Recommended Position)
**Location:** After the introduction, before diving into the 15 signs
**Purpose:** Capture readers early and provide immediate value
**Content Context:**
```markdown
## Assess Your Automation Readiness - Interactive Checklist

Before diving into the signs, take our comprehensive automation readiness assessment. This interactive tool will evaluate your current state across three critical areas and provide personalized recommendations based on your specific situation.

**Complete the assessment below to get your automation priority score and customized action plan:**

[interactive type="automation-checklist"]

Based on your assessment results, you'll understand exactly where automation can have the biggest impact on your business operations.
```

### Benefits of This Placement:
- Engages readers immediately with interactive content
- Provides personalized context for the rest of the article
- Higher completion rates due to early engagement
- Qualifies leads before they read solutions

- No additional configuration needed
- Automatic lead tracking through existing analytics
